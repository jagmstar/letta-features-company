# Fictional Case Study: Northstar Services and Letta Schedules

## Client profile
**Company:** Northstar Services  
**Industry:** B2B professional services  
**Team size:** 24 people  
**Problem:** Too many reminders were trapped in inboxes, spreadsheets, and calendar nudges. Follow-ups were slipping, and account managers were spending time manually tracking who to contact next.

## The challenge
Northstar’s team needed a way to:
- send recurring client follow-ups
- remind account managers about renewals
- keep weekly status checks consistent
- reduce time spent manually updating reminder lists

Before Schedules, each manager handled follow-ups differently. Some used calendar reminders, some used task apps, and others relied on memory. The result was inconsistency, missed touches, and wasted admin time.

## The solution
Northstar adopted **Letta Schedules** to create a repeatable follow-up workflow:
- daily client check-ins
- renewal reminders 30 days before contract end
- weekly internal status summaries
- recurring project nudges for open action items

Because schedules live in the assistant workflow, the team could set the task once and let Letta bring it back later with the original context.

## Before / after

| Metric | Before | After |
|---|---:|---:|
| Time spent managing reminders | 8 hours/week | 1.5 hours/week |
| Missed follow-ups | 10+ per month | 1–2 per month |
| Renewal reminders tracked manually | Spreadsheet + calendar | Native schedules |
| Follow-up consistency | Variable by manager | Standardized workflow |
| Leadership visibility | Low | High |

## ROI calculation

### Assumptions
- 24 people, but only 8 account managers use Schedules regularly
- Schedules saves **9.5 hours/week** across the team
- Loaded labor cost: **$60/hour**
- Monthly subscription cost: **$750/month**

### Monthly value created
- 9.5 hours/week × 4.33 weeks/month = **41.13 hours/month**
- 41.13 hours × $60/hour = **$2,468/month** in time saved

### Annualized value
- $2,468 × 12 = **$29,616/year**

### Net ROI
- Net monthly value = $2,468 - $750 = **$1,718/month**
- ROI = (($2,468 - $750) / $750) × 100 = **229.1% monthly ROI**

### Optional upside
If Schedules helps recover just **one mid-market renewal** or one delayed deal every quarter, the revenue impact can dwarf the labor savings.

## Customer outcome
After a 30-day pilot, Northstar reported:
- fewer missed renewals
- faster client follow-up times
- less manual reminder management
- better confidence that recurring tasks would happen on schedule
