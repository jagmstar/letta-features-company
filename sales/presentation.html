Slide 4 · How we work

Six rounds. Twenty-four role switches. Parallel agents.

Operating model
Round 1

Set the baseline

The first pass establishes the research, architecture, QA, and delivery lanes so work can proceed in parallel without blocking the team.

  • Research investigates feature opportunities
  • Development starts implementation
  • QA begins validation paths
  • DevOps readies the infrastructure
Round 2

Switch roles, keep momentum

Each digital twin changes responsibilities instead of waiting around. That creates a resilient team that can cover product, security, sales, writing, and architecture needs.

Round 3–6

Repeat the loop until the demo is ready

Across six rounds, the system continues to research, build, test, document, and refine in parallel. The full operating pattern produced 24 role switches and real output each round.

  • Parallel work across multiple agents
  • Role rotation after each completed task
  • Delivery focused on working artifacts, not just plans
Parallel execution is the competitive advantage. No idle agents.
