# Letta Schedules
## Set it now, get it back at the right time.

**Letta Schedules** is the time-based follow-up feature that turns reminders, check-ins, and recurring tasks into dependable workflows.

### Why teams buy it

- ⏰ **Never miss a follow-up** — schedule the action once and let Letta bring it back later.
- 🧠 **Keep context attached** — the follow-up returns with the original intent, not just a generic alert.
- 🚀 **Make the assistant proactive** — Letta can reach back out automatically for briefs, renewals, and recurring checks.
