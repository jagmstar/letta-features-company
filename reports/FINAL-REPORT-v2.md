# Digital Twin Company — Final Report

## Executive Summary

Digital Twin Company delivered three connected product pillars: **Schedules** for proactive follow-ups, **Skills** for reusable capability packaging, and **Channels** for operating inside Slack, Telegram, and Discord. Together they form a compact but complete assistant platform with a production scheduler, REST API, dashboard, file-backed runtime state, Docker/Compose packaging, GitHub Actions CI/CD, and a published dashboard on GitHub Pages.

The team operated like a real multi-department company rather than a single feature sprint. Work was spread across five rounds of parallel execution, with agents reused across roles instead of replaced, which preserved continuity between research, product, architecture, engineering, QA, sales, documentation, and operations. That structure produced a coherent story: earlier research informed the roadmap, engineering shipped the core surfaces, QA hardened the edge cases, and sales/documentation packaged the result for Roman.

The next phase is less about invention and more about hardening and scaling. The roadmap now points toward durable shared state, stronger schema validation, better secrets handling, automated dashboard regeneration, and broader channel/skill orchestration. In short: the product is built, the operating model worked, and the remaining work is to make the platform more resilient, more portable, and easier to extend.
